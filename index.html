Camp China 2018

Applications are now open!

Welcome to Camp China! Are you ready to experience the most life-changing journey ever in Asia? Created by the award-winning Camp Thailand team, we are a trusted name within the industry with years of experience behind us.

With each member of the team holding travel and Asia close to their hearts, here at Camp China we know what’s important to each one of our applicants. We are there for each and every one of them every step of the way to ensure that everyone gets the best out of their time in China.

This trip will take place in May 2018 to July 2018. The average duration of your stay will be around 3 weeks. Packages are from £760.

About Us Information about what we do and Contact Information

Camp.co.uk is committed to providing you with the world’s best and most exciting camp locations; wherever you would like to visit, we can do our very best to get you there. We have a variety of camp locations and opportunities on every single continent, with chances for a summer experience or even a gap year.

Whether you want to be involved with Camp Thailand, AmeriCamp, Camp South Africa or even AusJob, our team is committed to helping you on your journey. We specialise in recruiting people from all across the world, who are passionate about making a positive impact on not only their own lives, but to the lives of people from local communities and wildlife at whichever camp location they choose.

We live by the philosophy: “Work Hard, Play Hard and Make A Positive Impact!”

With camp locations in over 20 countries, the most difficult question is: Which one to do first? Would you prefer to chat over the phone? All of our team are passionate about travel (and talking!) so we’re always up for a chat!

Give us a call on (+44) 0161 222 3780!
